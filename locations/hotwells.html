Hotwells is one of Bristol's most distinctive neighbourhoods, situated at the foot of Clifton and stretching along the historic harbourside. The area takes its name from the hot springs that once attracted visitors in the 17th and 18th centuries, and today it remains a sought-after residential location with a unique character. The neighbourhood features a fascinating mix of elegant Georgian townhouses, Victorian terraces, and modern harbourside apartments converted from former industrial buildings.

The diverse property types in Hotwells present equally varied plumbing challenges. Our plumbers have extensive experience working throughout the area and understand the specific requirements of each building style. From maintaining original Georgian lead pipework and managing gravity-fed systems in period properties to servicing modern unvented cylinders in contemporary apartments, we have the skills and knowledge to handle any plumbing situation you may encounter.

  • Boiler Repair and Servicing - Our Gas Safe registered engineers service and repair all boiler makes and models.
