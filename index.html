Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to a plumbing emergency in Bristol?

We aim to reach most Bristol locations within 30-60 minutes of your call. Our emergency plumbers are strategically located across Bristol to ensure the fastest possible response times. During peak periods, we'll always give you an honest estimate of our arrival time.

Do you charge extra for emergency callouts at night or weekends?

Our rates remain competitive regardless of when you call. While emergency callouts may have different pricing to standard appointments, we always provide transparent quotes before beginning any work. You'll never face unexpected charges on your bill.

Are your plumbers Gas Safe registered?

Yes, all our plumbers who work on gas appliances are fully Gas Safe registered. We can provide registration details upon request for complete peace of mind. Safety is our absolute priority on every job we undertake.

What should I do while waiting for the emergency plumber?

If you have a water leak, locate your stopcock and turn off the water supply to prevent further damage. For gas-related emergencies, turn off the gas at the meter if safe to do so and open windows for ventilation. Our phone team can provide specific guidance based on your situation.

Do you provide written quotes before starting work?

Absolutely. We always provide a clear, written quote before commencing any work. This quote will detail the work required, all costs involved, and the expected timeframe. We won't start work until you've approved the quote and you understand exactly what you're paying for.
